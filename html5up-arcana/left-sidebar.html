About Us

Hi! We are MAK Techie, a group of three girls who have way too much homework.

Homework is one of our BIGGEST problems. We never write it in our homework diaries(because they're at home all the time), and we've tried every homework planning method, only to end in unflattering detentions.

So? What's in it for you?

Well, we've decided to end this problem that school students suffer from, by creating AfterMath, the best web app for remembering homework tasks. Simply enter your task, a description, subject and phone number and AfterMath will send you a text message to remind you of your oncoming assignments. It's fast, affordable, easy-to-use, and a great help to students everywhere. Sign up today, and help plan your homework!
